How to Use the App

Welcome to the Stock App! Here’s a quick guide on how to use the app:

  • Portfolio: View your current balance and stock holdings.
  • Trade: Search for stocks to buy or sell.

Stock Trading Basics

Understanding how to buy and sell stocks is key to using this app effectively. Here’s a brief overview:

To buy stocks, follow these steps:

  • Go to the 'Trade' page.
  • Enter the stock symbol you want to buy.
  • Enter the quantity of stocks you want to purchase.
  • Click 'Buy' to complete the transaction.

To sell stocks, follow these steps:

  • Go to the 'Trade' page.
  • Enter the stock symbol you want to sell.
  • Enter the quantity of stocks you want to sell.
  • Click 'Sell' to complete the transaction.
